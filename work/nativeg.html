NatiVeg

NatiVeg is a web application that helps land managers choose the proper native vegetation to plant based on their location and their use for planting the native vegetation. I coordinated the development of this application by translating client needs to fellow developers, and I also aided in the development of the SQL databases/geodatabases as well as the ASP.NET MVC application that is NatiVeg.
